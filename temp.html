Unit Conversion
Temperature


To convert Celsius to Fahrenheit yourself, use this formula replacing the "C" with your temperature in Celsius: (C x 9/5) + 32
Weight




To convert Kilogram to Pounds yourself, use this formula replacing the "kg" with your weight in Kilogram: kg x 2.205
Distance




To convert Kilometer to Mile yourself, use this formula replacing the "km" with your distance in Kilometer: km ÷ 1.609.
